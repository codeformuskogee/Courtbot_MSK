What is CourtBot?

PTA meetings, doctor's appointments, even court dates when life gets hectic, reminders help. COURTBOT is a text-message app that helps keep everything on track. After cases are booked, clients can text their case number to COURTBOT to receive automatic reminders of court dates.

Why CourtBot?

LESS BUREAUCRACY

Around 25% of the people in Tulsa County Jail are waiting for their cases to be heard, costing taxpayers up to $25,000 per day. Research shows that reminders work. More small fines are collected, and jail space is freed for more serious offenders.

FEWER FINES

Failure-to-appear and failure-to-pay fines can quickly snowball, especially for those living paycheck-to-paycheck. CourtBot helps clients avoid mistakes that burden them and the criminal justice system. Government done well can help.
